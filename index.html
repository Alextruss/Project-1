About Me

Based in Mid-Wales, surrounded by natural beauty I have always had an interest in Web Design and Photography. Although my life took a different direction and I ended up working in retail management, I have always wanted to push myself back into my first love, Web design and digital arts. From my first website built through trial and and error and examining source code, I was hooked. Weeks and months spent lost in tables and setting fonts to Comic Sans taught me the basics, but I always knew I wanted to know more.

With limited access to resources and no access to any academic tuiton, I taught myself everything i knew through trial and error and reading online tutorials to help develop my coding knowledge.

I attended Swansea college to study Interactive Digital Multimedia, and graduated with a HND. In my time there I studied:

  • Video Editing
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Flash Action Script
  • Image editing using Photoshop and Illustrator

In recent years I have been working in my spare time on various Wordpress and Joomla projects.
